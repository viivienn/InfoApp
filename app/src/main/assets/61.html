• Ensure, if possible, that the resident to remove their dentures at night 
  • Soak the dentures in denture cleaning solution at night to clean off bacteria
  • Brush the dentures the following morning to clean any remaining bacteria
  • Material needed
    1. Toothpaste
    2. A toothbrush specific to denture cleaning
    3. A regular toothbrush 

    Steps:
    1. Wash your hands and wear gloves 
    2. Place a washcloth at the bottom of the sink to avoid breaking the dentures if you drop them in the sink
    3. Use cold/lukewarm water â›” NOT hot water, which could damage the dentures
    4. Wet the dentures before applying toothpaste 
    5. Clean the inside of the dentures with the small head of the toothbrush specific to cleaning the dentures 
    6. To clean the teeth, you can use the large head of the specific toothbrush or a regular toothbrush
    7. Rince the dentures to ensure there is no toothpaste left
