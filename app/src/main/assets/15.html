In the case where a resident falls:

  • If you are close to the resident, try and break the fall by using your body.

  • Protect yourself from injury by standing with feet wide apart and knees slightly bent 

  • Always try to prevent the resident’s head from hitting the floor or any other surface

  • Stay with the resident and call for help from another professional

  • You may provide a blanket for the resident’s comfort while waiting for help to arrive 

  • DO NOT attempt to lift the resident off the floor by yourself. Wait for assistance

  • DO NOT raise the resident’s head as they may have a head or back injury
