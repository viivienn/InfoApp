• Have essentials within reach:

    • Walking aids (cane or walker) are placed within the patient’s reach at all times
    • Glasses, hearing aid, and phone are within reach on the bedside table
    • Call bell is placed within reach

  • Check for the patient’s level of independence in mobility 

    •  Often indicated on the whiteboard above the bed, pinned to the wall, or written in the patient’s files
    •  If the information is not available, refer to a staff member for guidance
