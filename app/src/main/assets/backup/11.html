Ensure:

  • No clutter on the floor (clothes, shoes, rolling table, wheelchair, etc.) so that all walkways are clear

  • No phone cords on the floor 
    •  If not, tuck them away or pass them behind the bed, close to the walls

  • Spills on the floor are cleaned up right away and look out for wet floors



  • Sheets and bedding are tucked into the mattress and not dangling onto the floor


  • Room is well lit by opening the lights or opening the drapes
