  • Wheelchair at 90° before eating 
  • Headrest adjusted to bring head in slight flexion 
  • Add tabletop if needed 
  • If the resident's head is too forward (chin almost at chest), tilt the wheelchair slightly back for better access to the mouth. Ensure the head is still forward.

    • Positioning in bed

  • Raise head of the bed in a comfortable seated position 
  • Place a pillow under the head and chest to help with swallowing, if needed
  • Head at 30-40° forward from the trunk 
  • Arms relaxed on either side, forearms on bed or on table 
  • Back straight and supported - not slanted or twisted to one side
  • Knees slightly bent (supported with pillows under/on the side for additional support )
  • Place something at the base of feet and below knees to support
