When assisting with bathing in bed:

  • Ask for consent before starting/continuing
  • Start from areas that are less private to build trust with the resident  (e.g. arms, legs) 
  • Respect the privacy and dignity of the resident
    • Turn the lights off if the resident feels more comfortable
    • Close the curtains and the door
    • Assist on the cleaning with the orderly if the resident consent
    • Cover parts of their body that do not need to be exposed
  • Look out for redness on the resident’s skin. If there is, advise your supervisor
  • When drying the resident’s skin, use a dry towel 
    • Remember to use patting motion instead of rubbing
    • Ensure that skin is completely dry and not humid
