Please advise a healthcare professional immediately upon suspicion of pressure wound. Interventions during the early stages of pressure sores can substantially prevent further complications. 

Signs of pressure sores during the early stage:

  • Redness, discoloured or darkened area of skin (blue or purple tones in people of dark complexion)
    • If the reddened or discoloured area does not return to normal within 10-30 minutes, this is often an indication of pressure sores
  • Sensitive to touch
  • Swelling
  • Scratches, blisters and calluses
  • Differs in temperature (warmer or cooler) compared to other areas of skin
