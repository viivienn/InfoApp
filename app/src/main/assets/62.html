Raise the head of the bed to 90° so the resident is sitting with their back straight
Materials needed:
  • A toothbrush
  • Toothpaste
  • A cup of water
  • An empty recipient

If the resident is able to brush their teeth: simply provide supervision 
  • Offer a glass of water and have them spit into the recipient 

If they are not able to: 
  1. Place one arm behind their neck and open their lower jaw using your index and thumb 
  2. With the other hand, brush all of their teeth
  3. Remove your arm carefully, offer them a sip of water, have them swish the water in their mouth, then spit into the recipient
