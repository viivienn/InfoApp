To ensure safety in bed
  • Bed should be kept at the lowest position
  • Wheels of the ebd must be locked at all times (pedals are down)
    •  The pedal brakes (lock/unlock) are often found at the bottom of the bed
  • Call bell/telephone are within reach of the bed
  • Bed rails are up at all times except on side where residents get out of bed
  • Power cords are not going through the bed rails

    • Elevation and inclination of the bed, leg and supports and backrest 

    Manual beds: adjust by turning a crank or by pulling the black lever (refer to picture)
