How to change a diaper in bed:
For residents who are bedridden, it will be necessary to change their diaper in bed
  1. Close the drapes and the door to ensure privacy
  2. Put on a clean pair of gloves
  3. Ensure the head of the bed is lowered so the resident is laying flat on their back
  4. Unwrap the front of the diaper and clean genitals, while observing skin condition 
  5. Using PDSB technique (refer to module 2), roll the resident onto their side so their back is facing you
  6. Clean the backside and check skin condition 
  7. Roll the diaper and dispose of it appropriately
  8. Change to a new pair of gloves
  9. Tuck in a new protective sheet and a new diaper under the resident who is lying on their side
  10. Apply moisturizing cream or powder to the backside, if needed
  11. Reposition the resident on their back 
    • Apply cream or powder on the front side, if needed
  12. Fasten diaper securely
    • Ensure that the diaper is not scrunched up or too tight to prevent pressure sores
  13. Dispose of soiled items appropriately
